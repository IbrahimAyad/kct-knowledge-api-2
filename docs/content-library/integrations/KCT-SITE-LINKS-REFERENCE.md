# KCT Menswear - Site Links Reference

> **For SEO Blog Agent Use** - Internal linking guide for subtle, elegant content integration

---

## Linking Philosophy

**Less is more.** Every link should feel like a helpful suggestion, not a sales pitch.

### Core Principles:
- **Reader-first**: Only link when it genuinely adds value
- **Natural flow**: Links should feel like organic recommendations
- **Trust-building**: Be helpful, not pushy
- **Strategic restraint**: 2-3 site links maximum per blog post

## Elegant Linking Guidelines

### Quantity Rules
- **Maximum 2-3 site links per blog post**
- Product links from `blog_product_assignments` are separate
- Never link the same page twice in one article

### Placement Strategy

**Opening (1 link max):**
> "Whether you're preparing for [a black-tie gala](/black-tie) or simply elevating your everyday style..."

**Body (1 link max):**
> "For those uncertain about measurements, our [size guide](/size-guide) offers precise guidance..."

**Conclusion (1 link max):**
> "Explore our [tailoring services](/tailoring) to ensure the perfect fit."

### Anchor Text Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- "our [size guide](/size-guide) provides detailed measurements"
- "consider [tailoring services](/tailoring) for a custom fit"
- "explore [wedding collections](/weddings/collections) for coordinated looks"

❌ **Don't:**
- "[Click here](/size-guide) for sizing"
- "Check out our [suits](/suits) [blazers](/blazers) [vests](/vests)"
- "SHOP NOW at our [store](/suits)!!!"

### Natural Integration Examples

**Subtle & Elegant:**
> "The key to looking sharp at any event lies in understanding fit. For those new to suiting, our [size guide](/size-guide) breaks down measurements in simple terms—no tailor's tape required."

**Too Promotional:**
> "BUY our amazing suits! Check out our [suits](/suits), [blazers](/blazers), and [accessories](/accessories)! Visit our [locations](/locations) TODAY!"

## Pages to AVOID Linking

These pages should never be linked in blog content:

- `/admin/*` - Admin pages
- `/login`, `/signup`, `/auth/*` - Authentication
- `/account/*` - User account pages
- `/checkout`, `/cart` - Transaction pages
- `/test-*`, `/atelier` - Development pages
- `/secret-products` - Hidden pages

## Integration with Product Links

Site links work alongside product links from `blog_product_assignments`:

```
Blog Post Structure:
├── Opening → 1 site link (context-setting)
├── Body
│   ├── Product callout (from blog_product_assignments)
│   ├── 1 site link (supporting resource)
│   └── Product gallery (from blog_product_assignments)
└── Conclusion → 1 site link (next step/CTA)
```

**Total Links Per Post:**
- 2-3 site links (from this reference)
- 2-4 product links (from `blog_product_assignments`)
- = 4-7 total internal links (subtle, strategic, valuable)
