## Common Alterations Explained

### **Pants & Trousers Alterations:**

**Hemming:**
- **What it is:** Shortening pants to proper length
- **Original hem:** Preserving manufacturer's hem finish
- **Plain hem:** Standard hem finish
- **Cuff hem:** Adding or maintaining cuff
- **Typical cost:** $14-28
- **Turnaround:** 3-5 days (same-day available)

**Waist Adjustments:**
- **Taking in:** Reducing waist size (typically up to 2-3 inches)
- **Letting out:** Expanding waist size (depends on fabric availability)
- **Split waistband method:** More precise adjustment
- **Remove waistband method:** For larger adjustments
- **Typical cost:** $15-24
- **Turnaround:** 5-7 days

**Tapering:**
- **What it is:** Narrowing leg width from knee to ankle
- **Modern slim fit:** Popular for contemporary look
- **Maintains proportions:** Keeps original design intent
- **Typical cost:** $28
- **Turnaround:** 5-7 days

**Seat Adjustments:**
- **Taking in:** Reducing excess fabric in seat/hip area
- **Letting out:** Adding room in seat/hip area
- **Typical cost:** $24
- **Turnaround:** 5-7 days

### **Suit & Jacket Alterations:**

**Sleeve Shortening/Lengthening:**
- **What it is:** Adjusting sleeve length for proper fit
- **Standard length:** Sleeve should end at wrist bone
- **Shirt cuff should show:** 1/4 to 1/2 inch of shirt cuff visible
- **Button placement:** We reattach buttons to new position
- **Typical cost:** $34 per jacket
- **Turnaround:** 5-7 days

**Taking In/Letting Out:**
- **Side seams:** Most common adjustment for fit
- **Waist suppression:** Creating tapered silhouette
- **Chest adjustment:** For better shoulder-to-waist fit
- **Typical cost:** $28
- **Turnaround:** 7-10 days

**Shoulder Adjustments:**
- **Complex alteration:** Requires jacket reconstruction
- **Padding adjustment:** Adding or removing shoulder pads
- **Slope adjustment:** Correcting shoulder angle
- **Typical cost:** $50-75
- **Turnaround:** 10-14 days

### **Dress Alterations:**

**Hemming:**
- **Simple hem:** Straight hem on plain fabric
- **Formal hem:** Multiple layers, lining, and outer fabric
- **Curved hem:** Following dress shape
- **High-low hem:** Asymmetrical hem
- **Typical cost:** $20-50 (depending on complexity)
- **Turnaround:** 5-7 days

**Taking In/Letting Out:**
- **Bodice adjustments:** Bust, waist, and hip
- **Dart manipulation:** Improving fit through darts
- **Side seam adjustments:** Most common method
- **Typical cost:** $25-40
- **Turnaround:** 7-10 days

**Strap Adjustments:**
- **Shortening straps:** Common for dresses that slip
- **Adding straps:** Creating support where none existed
- **Converting straps:** Halter to regular, strapless to straps
- **Typical cost:** $15-25
- **Turnaround:** 3-5 days

### **Zipper Replacement:**

**Why Zippers Need Replacement:**
- Teeth broken or missing
- Slider not catching teeth
- Zipper pulls off track
- Zipper fabric worn or torn
- Zipper too short or too long

**Types of Zippers We Replace:**
- **Pants zippers:** Most common replacement
- **Jacket zippers:** Including separating zippers
- **Dress zippers:** Invisible or standard
- **Coat zippers:** Heavy-duty zippers
- **Bag/purse zippers:** Specialty hardware

**Zipper Replacement Process:**
- Remove old zipper
- Select matching or upgraded replacement
- Install new zipper with reinforced stitching
- Test functionality
- Press and finish

**Typical cost:** $20-48 (depending on garment type)
**Turnaround:** 3-5 days (same-day available for simple replacements)
