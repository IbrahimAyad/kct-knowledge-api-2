## Section 4: Silhouette Evolution 2026

### **The Dominant Fit: Tapered, Tailored, Clean (Not Boxy, Not Skinny)**

**2026 Fit Philosophy**: "Structured elegance with ease of movement" = sharp silhouette with comfort[web:244][web:246]

**What's IN 2026:**
- ✅ **Tapered trousers** (fitted thigh, gradual taper to ankle)[web:244]
- ✅ **Structured shoulders** (defined but not heavily padded)[web:244]
- ✅ **Fitted jackets** (follow body shape, not tight)[web:244]
- ✅ **Shorter jacket lengths** (modern proportion, not 1990s long)[web:244]
- ✅ **Clean streamlined silhouettes** (no excess fabric)[web:244]

**What's OUT 2026:**
- ❌ **Boxy oversized suits** (dated 1990s/2000s)
- ❌ **Skinny ultra-tight fits** (dated 2010s, uncomfortable)
- ❌ **Long jackets** (unless Bridgerton-specific styling)

### **Three-Piece Suit Renaissance (HUGE 2026 Trend)**

**Why Three-Piece = Essential 2026:**
- **Bridgerton aesthetic**: Three-piece = period-accurate Regency tailoring (vests essential to era)[web:242][web:243]
- **Versatility**: Jacket on = formal // Jacket off, vest on = polished all day[web:244]
- **Visual interest**: Vest adds layer, dimension, sophistication[web:244]
- **Photography**: Multiple outfit options within one look (with jacket, without jacket)[web:244]

**Three-Piece Styling 2026:**

**Matching Three-Piece (Classic Bridgerton):**
- **What it is**: Jacket + pants + vest all same fabric/color
- **Best for**: Bridgerton themes, traditional elegance, formal weddings
- **Colors**: Pale blue, emerald, burgundy, navy, champagne

**Contrasting Vest (Modern Twist):**
- **What it is**: Suit jacket/pants one color, vest complementary color
- **Examples**:
  - Pale blue suit + burgundy vest
  - Champagne suit + sage green vest
  - Navy suit + oxblood vest
- **Best for**: Modern grooms wanting personality within structure[web:244]

**Textured/Patterned Vest:**
- **What it is**: Solid suit + brocade/jacquard/patterned vest
- **Best for**: Adding luxury detail, Bridgerton opulence, visual interest[web:243][web:244]

**Double-Breasted Vest (New 2026 Trend):**
- **What it is**: DB vest worn with single-breasted jacket (or no jacket)
- **Runway influence**: SS26 shows featured DB vests as statement pieces[web:244]
- **Best for**: Fashion-forward grooms, second look (reception outfit change)

**Three-Piece Market Prediction**: 40-45% of 2026 grooms (up from 30-35% in 2024-2025)[web:244]
